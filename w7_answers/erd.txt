ERD for the Airplane Ticket Booking System

Passengers can purchase multiple Tickets.
Each Ticket is associated with one Flight.
Each Flight has multiple Tickets sold.
ERD Relationships:

Passenger ⟷ Ticket → One-to-Many (1:M)
Flight ⟷ Ticket → One-to-Many (1:M)
Passengers ⟷ Flights (Many-to-Many through Tickets)
